Ask natural-language questions about Texter right inside the docs. A new right-docked chat panel, grounded in the full documentation, is now live on every page.

<!-- truncate -->

## Added

- **Ask AI panel** — click the "Ask AI" button in the bottom-right to open a chat that answers questions about YAML, adapters, scenarios, bot flows, and anything else covered in the docs. Works in both English and Hebrew.
- **Source citations** — every answer lists the docs pages it drew from, as clickable links that take you straight to the relevant reference.
- **Multi-turn follow-ups** — the panel remembers your conversation within the session so you can ask follow-ups like "and for Zoho?" without restating context.
- **"Ask about this page"** — one-click prompt in the empty state that asks the AI to summarize the page you're currently on, the key YAML points to follow, and common mistakes to avoid.
- **Prompt suggestions** — the empty state surfaces three rotating starter questions drawn from a pool of 30, covering data injection, Flows, adapters, prompts, notifies, working hours, and more.
- **YAML answers ready to paste** — YAML code blocks in answers are auto-indented by two spaces so you can paste them directly under an existing key in the bot editor.
- **Resizable panel** — drag the left edge to widen or narrow the panel; your preferred width is remembered across sessions.
